# RP volume landmarks — provenance & primary sources

Companion to `RP-volume-landmarks_current.csv` / `RP-volume-landmarks_current.md`.

## What this is
Per-muscle training **volume landmarks** (MV / MEV / MAV / MRV, plus the priority variants
MAV\*P / MRV\*P) in sets/muscle/week, **scraped directly from the live Renaissance Periodization
per-muscle articles on 2026-06-07.** Practitioner guidance from Dr. Mike Israetel / RP — **not
peer-reviewed**; treat as common-knowledge / starting-point data.

## History (why this replaced an earlier file)
An earlier version of this folder vendored a community aggregation
(github.com/vicentematus/volume-landmarks-rp, ≈2022 snapshot). RP has since **revised the
numbers** and added the MAV\*P / MRV\*P columns, so that aggregation was stale and has been
**deleted and replaced** with the fresh scrape above.

## Definitions (per RP)
- **MV** Maintenance Volume — keep what you have.
- **MEV** Minimum Effective Volume — least volume for measurable growth.
- **MAV** Maximum Adaptive Volume — average volume for best long-term gains.
- **MRV** Maximum Recoverable Volume — most you can recover from.
- **\*P** ("primary priority") — the variant when that muscle is prioritized and others reduced.

All units are sets/muscle/week, for serious intermediate lifters (3–7 yrs training). RP revises
these periodically — re-scrape to refresh.
